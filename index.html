Waterloo Sparkling Water

Way to healthy life!

Our Waterloo Sparkling Water is free of fluoride, sodium, and other additives/impurities before we add our flavors and carbonate. The water used in Waterloo is sourced regionally. All our sparkling waters are less acidic than traditional soft drinks and juices, with the added benefit of no sugar, sweeteners, coloring, or artificial ingredients. We have 11 water flavors: Pineapple, Peach, Orange, Strawberry, Grape, Blueberry, Watermelon, Original, Lemon-Lime, Grapefruit and Black Cherry.
